Frequently Asked Questions

Does Pinly track my location? No. Location data is processed strictly on-device for geofencing triggers. We don't see your data.
Can I backup my data? Yes. Pinly supports iCloud backup/restore and local JSON export (Pro).
Is it free? Pinly is free to use. Optional "Pro" features (like Quiet Hours and custom geofence radius) support development.
