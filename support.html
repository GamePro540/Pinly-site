Common questions

  • Location reminders not firing: Ensure Location = “Always”, Background App Refresh enabled, and notifications allowed.
  • Quiet Hours: Check Settings → Quiet Hours and verify your schedule.
  • Restore Pro: Use “Restore Purchases” in the app (if available) or sign in with the same Apple ID.
